Study Club

Join the club!

Join a club dedicated to providing a space for students to study, encourage studying, and meeting new friends. Join a cluster of students within similar majors/colleges to study with or attend our general study sessions.

Be a part of a cluster!

Join a cluster and be a part of a study group filled with students with similar majors to yours. Earn points for your cluster and compete in the Cluster Cup!

Take on a leadership role!

There are countless opportunities to take on a leadership role. Run for an officer position, such as Vice President, Secretary, or Treasurer, or become a Chair or Vice Chair of a cluster!
